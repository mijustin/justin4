Many folks talk about pricing as a leveraging factor.

But it's often too simplistic:

"If I charge $5/mo I only make $60/yr. But if I charge $500/mo I'll make $6,000!"

What's missing from the picture:
- ease of acquisition
- purchase frequency
- retention
- overall resilience
